Bao's
Bao's is a terrific Asian restaurant that serves freshly-prepared gyoza dumplings and silky bao buns.

The bao buns are the signature dish, and definitely worth coming for: light, soft and oozing with flavour. Many Chinese restaurants in Barcelona serve bao buns that are too heavy or have a cardboard texture, so it is refreshing to find ones that are so good.

The gyoza are excellent, too: freshly prepared right on the counter.

In fact, there’s not a dish that we wouldn’t recommend. Definitely give the noodle dishes a try – highly flavoursome.

Prices are reasonable, though unfortunately portions are a little small. Still, it’s nice to order a few to share - you can always order more if you are still hungry!

The place isn’t hug, perhaps no more than half a dozen tables inside and a couple that are outside.

Staff are friendly and have a smattering of English.

This is a very good place for take-away, too.
