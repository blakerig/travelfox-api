Welcome to the travel app that you have all been waiting for!
This is not one that is powered by clever AI algorithms. Nor is it one were users are permitted to upload hundreds of fake reviews. The content for this app is provided entirely by our dedicated team of professional travel writers and reviewers.
The only thing that matters to us is you, the user. We want you to be able to trust what you read here. If an attraction falls below expectations, then we will be honest and say so. If a restaurant is not up to scratch, then we won’t include it.
Fake reviews cause a huge problem for both consumers and honest businesses. Consumers waste money on poor experiences. Honest businesses struggle against competitors who bolster their image with unfair coverage.
Yet most travel apps out there allow just this: misleading reviews that harm consumers and distort fair competition.
All of our content is 100% independent. Our researchers have visited every single place that we include within this app, often multiple times. We do not take any payment for favourable coverage.
All adverts are totally separate from editorial content. We will always indicate where content has been sponsored.
We want to be different. We believe that you want us to be too!
